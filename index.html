Learning about Mental Disorders

This website seeks to inform young people about what mental disorders are, the types there are, how they occur and how to treat them. By the end of this website, you will be able to understand people around you who have them.

Aspergers

Belongs to autism spectrum disorders group. Is a neurological disorder ranging on the spectrum.
